# dream-tracker
A simple social platform for measuring and documenting the duration and quality of sleep as well as the narratives experienced during rapid eye movement dreaming.

## Functionality
Users are able to
- create an account and sign in,
- create new posts and edit or remove their own posts,
- follow other users,
- see their own posts, as well as posts by other users,
- set visibility rules for each post (e.g. public, private, followers only),
- select relevant categories for their posts,
- search for posts by search queries, categories or statistics,
- view other users' profile pages, as well as their own, which display said user's posts and their statistics,
- reply to their own posts or posts published by other users, as well as like them,
- and add their own or others' posts to curated lists.
